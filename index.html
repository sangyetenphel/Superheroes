Iron Man

A billionaire inventor and genius who builds high-tech armored suits granting him flight, weapons, and super strength to protect the world as a charismatic, flawed hero.

Doctor Doom

The brilliant, iron-willed ruler of Latveria, armed with mystical knowledge and advanced armor. He seeks to conquer Earth to save it, viewing himself as its only worthy master.

Spider-Man

A young hero gifted with wall-crawling abilities and super strength after a radioactive spider bite. He fights crime while balancing ordinary life, guided by responsibility.

Deadpool

A wisecracking mercenary with accelerated healing and a twisted sense of humor. He breaks the fourth wall, embraces chaos, and delivers violent justice with comedic flair.

Hulk

A scientist whose gamma-radiation curse transforms rage into a giant, green engine of destruction. He struggles between Bruce Banner's intellect and the Hulk's uncontrollable power.
